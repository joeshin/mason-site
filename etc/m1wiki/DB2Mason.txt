DB2Mason is a framework and generator to simplify your development of database-based webapplications. It is based on HTML::Mason , Perl and of cause the database of your choice. You implement your database (tables, indexes, constraints, ...) and DB2Mason does the rest for you. 

Version 0.1.2 is released now.
New features are
* Support for Apache on Windows
* During installation on Oracle 10g the "database recyclebin" is considered
